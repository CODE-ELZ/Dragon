About Jackie Chan Adventures

Jackie Chan Adventures is a Chinese-American animated television series chronicling the adventures of a fictionalized version of action film star Jackie Chan. There are numerous references to Jackie Chan's films and his own life throughout the series. The series ran on Kids' WB! from September 9, 2000 to July 8, 2005 for a total of 95 episodes and 5 seasons. It was also shown on Cartoon Network and Disney XD. Books, comics, and two video games were released to accompany the series. Its main character designer is Jeff Matsuda.

Jackie Chan

The main protagonist a martial arts expert & an archeologist who secretly works for Section 13 , his mission, is to collect the Talismans and defeat the Demon Sorcerers as well.

Jade Chan

Jackie's 12-year-old niece from China. She moved to the United States and has enjoyed her adventures with her uncle, who believes the missions he goes on are too dangerous for Jade.

Uncle Chan

Jackie's uncle who owns an antique shop. He is a Chi Wizard and studied with Chi Master Fong for 15 years. He knows a lot about antiques and magical things, and does research to find out what magic forces Jackie may deal with in his adventures.

Tohru

As a large Japanese man, weighing approximately 480 pounds and standing taller than most. Tohru an Enforcer of the Dark Hand originally an antagonist , but ultimately choses to reform, becoming an indispensable ally to the Chans.

Villains

Goal is to gather all the magical Talismans in order to revive Demon Sorcerer possessing Valmont.

Tohru

Tohru is generally a rather slow person, but this does not mean that he is completely stupid, only that he goes rather easy on letting his mind do the hard work.

Finn

Finn with Irish background is the brains (or at least the most intelligent, or less stupid) of the Enforcers. He also known for his jokes which he tends to crack during missions.

Ratso

Ratso is above average in height, being slightly shorter than Hak Foo, is a nerdy but basically easy-natured strongman who displays child-like fears and desires, like a love for toys and sandwiches.

Chow

Being of Chinese descent,Chow often puts on a bold front, especially when confronted by Jackie. He is usually a good fighter in a battle, and is the most martial-arts efficient member of the Dark Hand (before Hak Foo came along).

Hak Foo

known as The Black Tiger. Hak Foo demonstrates a big ego, a short temper, and overconfidence to match; thus, Because of his abilities and mannerisms, he is very condescending to minors and eager to pick any chance for a fight.

Valmont

leader and founder of the Dark Hand and operates out of self-interest. Valmont is tall and athletic, generally intelligent and level-headed. He is arrogant and driven by greed (as shown by his desire to attain Shendu's lost treasure).

Shendu

a Demon Sorcerer (specifically a Fire Demon, a title which he shares with his future son, Drago), and is arguably the overarching primary antagonist of Jackie Chan Adventures Shendu's ultimate objective, having been trapped in his statue form for nine-hundred years since being deposed, was to retrieve all Twelve Talismans to restore him to his true form and reclaim his lost power, for which he utilised Valmont and his Enforcers.
